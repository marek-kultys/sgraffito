Trowel & Lime creates sgraffito.

Sgraffito is a beautiful traditional mural technique originating from ancient Rome and rediscovered in Renaissance. It is renowned for its durability, resilience to the elements and a monochromatic imagery. Sgraffito is a subtle way of giving the space texture, colour and light.

Explore the gallery to see our work or read our blog to discover historic examples of sgraffito.
