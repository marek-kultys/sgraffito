Trowel & Lime creates sgraffito murals — traditional wall decoration for indoor and outdoor spaces.

Made entirely by hand and based on unique designs, sgraffito is a special and uncompromising way to bring art into a living or working space.

Made to last

Sgraffito murals are made to last decades and longer.

We use traditional methods and tools, and traditional materials, such as lime render, natural pigments, pure sand, and marble dust.

Made to inspire

Popular in ancient Rome, sgraffito was rediscovered in Renaissance and later again by Art Nouveau.

Today, unique façade sgraffitos give Prague, Barcelona, and Kraków their very special character.

Trowel & Lime offers the art of sgraffito murals in London.
